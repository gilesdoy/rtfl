Prizes

There are three prizes for the top-ranking players in this year's Real Tennis Fantasy League.

The winner has first choice on two prizes from the prize pool.
The runner-up has second choice on one prize from the prize pot.

The prize pool contains the following:
  • One T-shirt and one tracksuit bottom from the 'Chasing the Hazard' catalogue
  • One T-shirt from the 'Chasing the Hazard' catalogue
  • One first edition copy of the Reste Board game.
