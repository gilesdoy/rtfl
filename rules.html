Rules

Remit

  1. Events within scope of the Real Tennis Fantasy League (RTFL) include
    • all 4 elite Open tournaments: French Open, British Open, Australian Open, US Open
    • the US Professional Singles (Newport)
    • the Real Tennis Champions Trophy (Hampton Court)
    • all 5 Tambour Tour Events in the UK.
  2. The RTFL covers the men's singles events only.
  3. For each event, participants may pick a squad of up to three players

Budget

  1. Each player has a price and you must spend within your budget for the season.
  2. The budget for the 2023/24 season is £225 million.
  1. Squad selections must be submitted through this website.
  2. The deadline for selections is one hour before the advertised start time of the first match of the event.
  3. The window for making squad selections generally opens either 21 days before the event, or immediately on the conclusion of the previous event, whichever is sooner.
  4. You may make unlimited squad changes up until the deadline. You can choose the same person in your squad multiple times.
  1. Participants receive points on the basis of their squad's performance. The table below sets out the points structure.
  2. Two bonuses are also available:
    • Participants score four points if any player in their squad achieves a 'Big Win' (W!) as according to RTO.
    • Any participant who picks a player that no-one else has picked, scores double points for that player. Double points also apply to the Big Win bonus, as described above.
  3. The most points at the end of the season is the winner.
  1. Budgets are never disclosed and there are no bonus points for any budget left over at the end of the season.
  2. This website will show which players have been selected:
    • in full once the deadline for an event has passed.
    • anonymously (as a bar chart) up to the event deadline.
